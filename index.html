About

I am interested in the intersection between art, electronics, communication, and the natural world.

I currently work as part of the Exhibits department at the Natural History Museum of Utah in Salt Lake City. I graduated from SUNY Adirondack with an AAS in Media Arts in 2014 before graduating magna cum laude from Purchase College with a BA in New Media in 2017.

This site documents my return to web development through EDPS 6560 — building accessible, static sites with semantic HTML, thoughtful CSS, and small touches of JavaScript.
