About

I am interested in the intersection between art, electronics, communication, and the natural world.

I am currently pursuing a Master of Education in Instructional Design & Educational Technology at the University of Utah, and work in the Exhibits department at the Natural History Museum of Utah in Salt Lake City.

Previously, I graduated from SUNY Adirondack with an AAS in Media Arts in 2014 before graduating from Purchase College with a BA in New Media in 2017.
